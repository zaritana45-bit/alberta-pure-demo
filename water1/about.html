About AlbertaPure

Clean water you can trust — right here in Alberta

Who We Are

We're a family-founded company based right here in Calgary, and we've been helping Alberta families enjoy better water for over 10 years.

Back when we started, hard water was just something everyone complained about — the white scale on faucets, the dry skin after showers, the funny taste from the tap. We got tired of hearing the same stories from friends and neighbors, so we decided to do something about it.

Today we're proud to say that thousands of homes across Alberta (and especially in Calgary and the surrounding areas) get their water through one of our systems. It's not just a business for us — it's personal.

Why Our Filters Stand Out

We don't sell the cheapest filters on the market, and that's by design. We choose systems that actually work well with Alberta's water — the high mineral content, occasional iron issues, and that stubborn hardness we all know too well.

Every filter we carry goes through rigorous testing. We only partner with manufacturers who use:

  • ✓ NSF/ANSI certified components (real certification, not just claims)
  • ✓ High-capacity media that lasts longer in hard-water conditions
  • ✓ BPA-free, food-grade materials throughout
  • ✓ Quiet, efficient designs that don't waste water unnecessarily

The result? Systems that quietly do their job for years, without constant headaches or surprise breakdowns.
